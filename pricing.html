Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in your billing.

What happens if I exceed my conversation limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional conversations at a discounted rate.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all new customers. No credit card required. Book a consultation to get started.

What integrations are supported?

We support integrations with all major CRMs, help desk platforms, e-commerce systems, and communication channels. Custom integrations are available for Business and Enterprise plans.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you choose annual billing. That's like getting more than 2 months free.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include email support. Professional plans and above receive priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers get 24/7 dedicated support with a dedicated account manager.
